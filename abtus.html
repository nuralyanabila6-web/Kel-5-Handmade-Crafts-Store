About Us

Atelier Kriya

At Atelier Kriya, we believe that every handmade piece carries a fragment of its creator’s soul. Born from a love for detail and the beauty of craft, we offer collections specifically designed to accompany your cherished moments. Each of our creations is a blend of imagination, patience, and sincere love. Founded in 2020 by a group of passionate artisans in Jakarta, Indonesia, Atelier Kriya started as a small workshop where traditional crafting techniques were revived. Today, we have grown into a beloved brand that celebrates the art of handmade goods, from knitted accessories and beaded jewelry to clay sculptures and floral wire crafts.

Our mission is to preserve the timeless beauty of craftsmanship while promoting sustainability, creativity, and community empowerment. We source materials ethically, ensuring that every thread, bead, and clay piece comes from responsible suppliers who prioritize fair trade and environmental care. Our team of skilled artisans—including expert knitters, beaders, sculptors, and designers—pours their expertise into each product, guaranteeing that no two items are exactly alike. We believe in empowering local communities by providing fair wages, skill-building workshops, and opportunities for growth, turning craft into a sustainable source of livelihood and pride.

At Atelier Kriya, we are more than just a store—we are a community of dreamers and makers. Our values revolve around authenticity, quality, and meaningful connections. Whether you're gifting a handmade knitted doll to a child, adorning your home with a clay photo frame, or personalizing your space with beaded accessories, our pieces are designed to bring joy, warmth, and inspiration to your life. We invite you to join us in celebrating the magic of handmade crafts, one stitch, bead, and mold at a time. Explore our collections and discover how our creations can add a touch of heart to your everyday moments.
